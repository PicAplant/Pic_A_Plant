Pic A Plant App

The last word in the world of plant identification applications. A powerful, reliable, accessible and fast plant identifier

Specifications :


  • Our application uses a smart component which consists of a neural network CNN-type, decodes an image and returns a result with the name of the plant and the identification percentage. You will be able to identify 15 thousand different species of plants!
  • The application is based on a smart infrastructure that provides fast service.
  • We believe in the privacy of your information security and the reliability of our app service. All activity in the application is secure and safe to use
  • Explore full plant species with smart text search and get detailed information about each plant!
  • Community and social sharing, sharing plant identification and messages in forum groups. You can open your own group or join an existing one.
  • You can enjoy a dynamic quiz that generates questions in real time and earn Leaf Coin

About App :

We are industrial engineering and management students majoring in information systems and as part of our final project was to build an information system. Our system consists of 3 parts:
Client side -
An easy to use mobile application with an accessible design for all ages.
Server side -
Smart algorithms and the use of smart and dynamic components give a reliable experience that is fast and safe to use.
The database -
Our database is managed from 20 tables and includes interaction between the tables for the transfer and refinement of reliable information
